Konafa with cream

the components


Kunafa dough, finely chopped Ghee or butter
food colouring
For the cream:
The first stage: 1 liter of milk + 2 tablespoons of white vinegar
The second stage: 1 cup of liquid milk + 2 tablespoons of starch + half a teaspoon of blossom water + half a teaspoon of rose water
For the drink:
1 cup of sugar
1/2 cup of water
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
Saffron can be added if desired
